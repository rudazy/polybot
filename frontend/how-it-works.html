How It Works

Welcome to the Polymarket Trading Bot - your automated trading assistant for prediction markets. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about using our platform.

What is Polymarket?

Polymarket is the world's largest prediction market platform where you can trade on the outcome of future events - from elections and sports to crypto prices and pop culture. Think of it as betting on future events, but with real money and instant liquidity.

Unlike traditional betting sites, Polymarket uses blockchain technology (Polygon) and lets you trade shares representing "YES" or "NO" outcomes. Prices reflect the crowd's prediction of probability, ranging from $0.01 (very unlikely) to $0.99 (very likely).

Example: Presidential Election

If "Will X win the election?" trades at $0.65, the market thinks there's a 65% chance. You can buy "YES" shares at $0.65 and sell them at $1.00 if X wins, making $0.35 profit per share.

How Trading Works

Manual Trading

The simplest way to trade. Browse markets or paste a Polymarket URL, then:

  • Select YES (you think it will happen) or NO (you think it won't)
  • Enter amount in USDC.e
  • Optionally set stop-loss and take-profit
  • Click "Execute Trade"

The bot calculates how many shares you'll get based on current prices and executes the trade on Polymarket Exchange.

Automated Bot Trading

Let the bot find and execute trades for you based on your criteria:

  • Category: Crypto, Politics, Sports, etc.
  • Probability Threshold: Only trade if market is above X% (e.g., 70%)
  • Amount Per Trade: How much USDC.e to spend per trade
  • Stop Loss: Exit if position drops by X%
  • Take Profit: Exit if position gains X%

The bot scans markets continuously and executes trades that match your criteria. You can stop it anytime.

Copy Trading

Follow successful traders automatically:

  • Browse top traders (shown with win rates and volumes)
  • Select a trader to copy
  • Set your max amount per trade
  • Start copy trading

When the trader you're following makes a trade, your bot makes the same trade (proportionally to your amount limit). Great for beginners!

Wallet & Security

Safe Wallet vs Regular Wallet

We use Safe Wallets (formerly Gnosis Safe) which offer several advantages:

  • Gasless Trading: No POL needed for each trade
  • Secure: Your private keys are encrypted in our database
  • Exportable: You can export your wallet keys anytime
  • Self-Custodial: You own the wallet, not us

What You Need

  • POL (Polygon): ~0.05 POL for gas fees (USDC approval, withdrawals)
  • USDC.e: Trading capital (start with $5-10 to test)

How to Get POL and USDC.e

  • Buy POL: Coinbase, Binance, Kraken, etc.
  • Bridge USDC: Use Polygon Bridge (wallet.polygon.technology/bridge)
  • POL Faucet: faucet.polygon.technology (small amounts)

Security Best Practices

• Never share your private keys
• Export and backup your wallet keys
• Start with small amounts to test
• Enable 2FA if available
• Only deposit what you can afford to lose

Frequently Asked Questions

What is USDC.e and why not native USDC?
USDC.e is "bridged USDC" - USDC that was bridged from Ethereum to Polygon. Polymarket uses USDC.e, not native USDC. Make sure you're using the correct token (0x2791Bca1f2de4661ED88A30C99A7a9449Aa84174).
Why do I need to approve USDC.e?
This is a standard blockchain process. You're giving permission to the Polymarket Exchange smart contract to spend your USDC.e. This is safe and required for all DEX trading. You only do it once.
Is unlimited approval safe?
Yes! Unlimited approval is industry standard (used by Uniswap, etc.) and only applies to the specific smart contract (Polymarket Exchange). It can't access other tokens or accounts. You can revoke it anytime on Polygonscan.
How do I withdraw my funds?
Click "Withdraw Funds" in your wallet section. Select token (USDC.e or POL), enter recipient address and amount. For Safe Wallets, your owner address needs ~0.1 POL for gas fees.
Can I use MetaMask instead?
Yes! You can connect an external wallet (MetaMask, Rabby, etc.) but you'll need POL for every transaction. Safe Wallets offer gasless trading which is more convenient.
What happens if the bot loses money?
Prediction markets carry risk just like any trading. Use stop-loss orders to limit losses. Start small, test the bot, and never invest more than you can afford to lose. Past performance doesn't guarantee future results.
How long do trades take to settle?
Trades execute instantly. Markets settle when the event resolves (could be hours, days, or months depending on the market). You can sell your position anytime before settlement.
Can I export my wallet?
Yes! Click "Export Safe Wallet Keys" to get your owner private key. You can import this into MetaMask or any wallet to control your Safe Wallet externally.
